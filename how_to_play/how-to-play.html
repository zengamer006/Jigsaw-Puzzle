Jigsaw Puzzle Game

Getting Started

  1. Open the game to see a scrambled image divided into pieces.
  2. Your task is to arrange the pieces to reconstruct the original image.

Playing the Game

  1. Drag and drop puzzle pieces to rearrange them.
  2. Answer the pop-up questions correctly to progress.
  3. Connect matching pieces by placing them next to each other.
  4. Continue until the image is completed.

Tips for Success

  • Start with the edges to create a frame.
  • Group similar colors or patterns together.
  • Pay attention to the connectors' shapes.
  • Take breaks if you're stuck to get a fresh perspective.
